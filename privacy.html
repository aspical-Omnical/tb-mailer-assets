Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how The Bidder Auctions ("The Bidder", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information in connection with our auction house, our website and communications, and our marketing activities. We are committed to handling personal data responsibly and in accordance with applicable law.

1. Who we are

The Bidder Auctions, Leibowitz 9, Gedera, Israel. Email: gallery@thebidderauction.com. Phone: 08-6446966. The Bidder is the entity responsible for (the controller of) the personal data described here.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide — name, email address, phone number.
  • Auction activity — registration, bidding, and purchase history on our catalogues (hosted via the Bidspirit platform).
  • Communication preferences and your interactions with our emails and ads.
  • Website and advertising data — limited technical and engagement data collected through cookies and advertising platforms when you visit our pages or view our ads.

3. How we use information

  • To operate our auctions and provide our services to you.
  • To send you sale announcements, catalogues, and related updates you have asked to receive.
  • To deliver, target, and measure our own advertising and marketing campaigns.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our services and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Advertising and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) platforms

We use Meta's advertising tools — including the Meta Marketing API and Custom Audiences — to create, manage, and measure advertising for our own business. Where we use customer lists for audience targeting, they are processed by Meta on our behalf in hashed form. Any data we obtain from Meta ("Platform Data") is used solely to operate and report on our own advertising and is not sold or shared with unrelated third parties. We do not transfer Platform Data to data brokers or use it for any purpose other than managing our own marketing.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate, such as our auction platform (Bidspirit) and our email delivery provider, and only as needed to provide our services, or where required by law.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, meet legal and accounting requirements, and support legitimate business purposes, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may unsubscribe from marketing at any time using the link in our emails or by contacting us. To exercise any right, contact gallery@thebidderauction.com.

8. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against loss, misuse, and unauthorised access.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact
